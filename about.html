Our origins

Built from the ground up

After arriving from South Korea, Kyung Ho (Kay) Kim founded Smile Cleaning Ltd with a simple belief: that reliability, attention to detail, and genuine care is what separates a truly trusted cleaning partner from an average one.

The business began with residential cleaning — long hours, hands-on work, and a growing reputation built entirely on word of mouth. As that reputation grew, so did the operation. Schools, ECE centres, and commercial spaces began approaching Kay because they'd heard the same thing from everyone: Smile Cleaning shows up, does the job properly, and never has to be asked twice.

Today, Smile Cleaning Ltd is a professional institutional cleaning company serving organisations across Auckland — but the values that started it haven't changed.

Kyung Ho (Kay) Kim
